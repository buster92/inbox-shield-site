Support

If you need help with Inbox Shield, this page provides basic support information and contact options.

Common questions

How do I analyze a message?
Open the Inbox Shield extension, paste or select the text you want to analyze, and click Analyze.

How do I rewrite a message?
After analyzing your message, use the Rewrite feature to generate an improved version.

Why do I see usage limits?
Some features depend on your current plan or available request quota.

How do I upgrade my plan?
Follow the upgrade option inside the extension interface.
